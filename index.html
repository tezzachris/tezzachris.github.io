Research Interests

My research is directed to stochastic processes and time series analysis. I work on statistical inference and asymptotic theory of time series models, stochastic models applied in derivatives pricing. My Ph.D. supervisors are Prof. Luca Vincenzo Ballestra and Dr. Enzo D'Innocenzo. During my master degree, I have worked on Bayesian Variational Approximations. My master supervisor was Prof. Matt Wand
