Research Interests

My research is directed to stochastic processes and time series analysis. I work on statistical inference & asymptotic theory of time series models and on stochastic processes applications in commodity markets. My Ph.D. supervisors are Luca Vincenzo Ballestra and Enzo D'Innocenzo. During my master degree, I have worked on Bayesian Variational Approximations under the supervision of Matt Wand and Luca Maestrini.
